The subdivision layer is derived from the subdivision title as shown on recorded plats.</div><div><br /></div><div>The subdivision layer was created to allow subdivisions to be queried and categorized both for the general public and for various departments within Loudoun County government. It serves as tool to monitor the Loudoun County Subdivision Ordinance.
